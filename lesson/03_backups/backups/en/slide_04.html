To create a simple backup of your android smartphone follow the following steps:

  1. Open your phone's Settings app
  2. Select Google and then Backup
  3. Tap "Back up now".

Your data is now backed up and can be restored in case of a defect, system restore or smartphone change.

Additionally, a cloud storage solution is recommended e.g. Dropbox, OneDrive, iCloud, pCloud or kDrive.
