True or false?

It takes longer to write encrypted e-mails than regular e-mails.



Once you have set up e-mail encryption, all you need to do is opt for “encrypt” in the e-mail window.

Encrypted e-mails are the safest option for data privacy in e-mail communication.



Encrypted e-mails ensure a much higher privacy than non-encrypted e-mails. If you want to be sure to be identified as sender, use a digital signature - in addition.

I can share my private key and public key with everyone.



Never share your private key – you are the only one to use this key for decrypting your personal e-mails. You only have to share your public key with the receiver.
