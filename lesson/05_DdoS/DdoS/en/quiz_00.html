True or false: A DDoS attack uses a network of sources to create an increased traffic on a target, for example a website.



DDoS stands for “distributed Denial of Service”. The traffic is distributed in a large network of devices.

DDoS or DoS attacks are usually meant to shut down the computer of an employee.



Such attacks usually tackle online services such as websites, online shops etc.

Which actions should you take when you experience a DoS/DDoS attack?




In case of a DDoS attack, get help from an expert and contact your web host or the concerned service. Turning off your computer won’t help. Another measure against DDoS is also the protection of websites by technical measures that the provider can offer.
