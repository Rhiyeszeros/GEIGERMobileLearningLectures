True oder false?

One hard drive as backup is sufficient.



Not quite! You need at least two hard drives that are in different locations so that they don't both come to harm. Also, consider disaster situations such as fires or floods. Also, if the hard drive is connected to the network, it may be subject to attacks such as ransomware.

I only need a backup in case my computer is broken.



A backup is also essential for restoring your data after a malware attack such as ransomware.

Backups are „nice-to-have".



Backups are vital to your cybersecurity and should not be neglected. The costs of losing access to data are much higher than the costs for regular backups. Since efficient and viable solutions are available, this risk can be easily avoided.
