True or false?

A strong password will ensure complete safety for my online accounts.



Even a strong password can be leaked, for example if the complete data base of an online service is hacked. Combine 2-Factor Authentication with a strong password to enhance the protection of your online accounts.

Two-factor authentication is only needed for my bank account.



2-Factor Authentication should be applied where possible, especially for e-mail accounts. After all, once others have access to your e-mail account, they might gain access to further online services.

A strong password and a PIN for entering an account is similar to 2-Factor-Authentification.



The 2 Factors should be of different kinds – password and PIN are both ‘knowledge of something’.
